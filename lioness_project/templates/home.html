We place crowns on women,
like queens

LIONESS is much more than a magazine.

It's a community born in Gabon that shines a spotlight on African and Afro-descendant women, both in Africa and in the diaspora, while remaining open to women worldwide.

We celebrate ambitious, entrepreneurial, creative, and committed women through inspiring stories, authentic journeys, and meaningful content.

Joining LIONESS, means joining a movement that celebrates women, their ambitions, and their impact on the world.
