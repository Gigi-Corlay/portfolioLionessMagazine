About LIONESS

LIONESS was created to give greater visibility to African and Afro-descendant women whose achievements are too often overlooked. Inspired by the remarkable women I met while growing up in Gabon, I wanted to build more than a digital magazine—I wanted to create a space where women could inspire one another, connect, and celebrate their success.

Developed as my Holberton School Portfolio Project, LIONESS combines my passion for web development with my commitment to promoting women's leadership. Built with Django, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it offers a modern, responsive, and accessible platform.
